Achievements

  • Awarded Formula Student Award worth 3000 GBP by Institution of Mechanical Engineers (IMechE) out of 82 teams internationally
  • Secured 4th position among all electric vehicles in Formula Student UK, the best rank by any Indian team
  • In-house fabricated light weight Carbon Fiber bodyworks
  • Implemented resin infusion for accumulator manufacturing for the first time in any car in the teams history
  • Implemented 3-D printed Titanium uprights, self made Aerodynamics package and built an in-house battery management system
  • High power Brushless DC Motors with Regenerative Braking were implemented to achieve 0-100 kmph in 3.10 sec
